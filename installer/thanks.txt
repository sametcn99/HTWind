Thank you for installing HTWind!

HTWind is now ready to transform your Windows desktop with custom HTML widgets.

Quick Start Guide:
- Locate the HTWind icon in your system tray (bottom-right corner).
- Use the "Add Widget" button to browse and add HTML widgets to your desktop.
- Toggle "Locked" mode on widgets to switch between moving/resizing and interacting.
- Use "Pin on top" for widgets you want to keep visible above other windows.

Create & Share:
- Build your own widgets using plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Use the built-in Editor with live preview to customize or create widgets from scratch.
- Join our community to share your amazing widgets or find new inspirations!
